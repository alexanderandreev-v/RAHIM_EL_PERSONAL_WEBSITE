About me

Hi, my name is Rahim. I am an artist from Moscow. I am engaged in art for more than 7 years. My main works are oil paintings, abstract painting, figurative. For me, human and color have always been the center of my work. I try to transfer the image of everyone who left a mark in my heart onto the canvas. In my paintings you can see the most hidden and unexpected sides of the human soul.

Comissions and purchases

If you want to buy the work that you saw on my website, or are interested in commissioning, or anything else, the best way to get started is to simply call or email me. We'll be in touch shortly.
